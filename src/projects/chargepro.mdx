## Overview

    ChargePro is a WordPress development project designed to promote electric vehicle charging services. The objective was to create a modern Elementor template kit that highlights charging station locations, pricing plans, benefits of EV adoption, and seamless booking options — all while reflecting the brand’s commitment to sustainability and innovation.
