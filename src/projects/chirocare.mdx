## Overview

    ChiroCare is a WordPress development project designed to create an informative and approachable website for a chiropractic clinic. The goal was to design an Elementor template kit that emphasizes the clinic's services, builds trust with potential patients, and makes appointment scheduling effortless. The design focused on creating a clean, welcoming, and professional user experience to reflect the clinic's dedication to holistic care and wellness.
